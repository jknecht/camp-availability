Shower building. Anyone registered to camp on any of the forest's campgrounds may use these facilities.
Flush toilets. All family campgrounds and group campgrounds have vault toilets. Remote reservable and canoe sites have wilderness pit latrines. In addition four campgrounds have flush toilets.
